Exploring the wilderness,

Conquering the Brazil

This site is one of products that resulting of research "Exploring the wilderness, conquering the Brazil: the Exploration Roncador Xingu and the Brasil Central Fundation in Mato Grosso state (1943-1967)", a investigation about the methods of the Roncador-Xingu Expedition and the action of the Brasil Central Fundation in the Villas-Boas Brothers Expedition, occoured in the est of Mato Grosso state, in the Araguaia Valley region. The teacher Ms. Luiz Gabriel de Souza Nogueira oversaw the investigation process that had the participation of students of the 3° Sgt PM Justino Pinheiro dos Santos - Tiradentes Military State School, situed in Água Boa county, Mato Grosso state. This research received financial support of Mato Grosso State Research Support Fundation - (Fapemat).
